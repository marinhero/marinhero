About me

Hello! I’m Marín Alcaraz. Right now, I work as a Full Stack Engineer for Wootric.

I studied Computer Systems Engineering at the ITESM with a brief, but awesome stint at EPITECH.

After, college I went to the Recurse Center and had the best time of my life building cool software projects, like a Dropbox clone or my own ad-blocker. I’m passionate about Infrastructure, Information Security, Ruby, and TypeScript.

When I’m away from my keyboard you can find me singing, dancing Rueda de Casino, or riding my bike. I dream of riding the Baja Divide one day.
